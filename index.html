ABOUT US

At A&C Garage Grub, we bring together the best of both worlds—a full-service garage and a cozy restaurant—all under one roof. Whether you’re here to get your vehicle serviced or just craving a delicious meal, we’ve got you covered. Our skilled mechanics ensure your ride stays in top shape, while our kitchen serves up hearty, satisfying grub made from fresh, quality ingredients. Drive in for expert care and stay for the friendly atmosphere and great food. At A&C Garage Grub, we fuel both your vehicle and your appetite!
